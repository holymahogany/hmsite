This site is a place to gather
my various projects.

I've illustrated magazine covers,
published an academic article
(link forthcoming Sep. 2022),
and created little videogames.

I'm interested in the aesthetics
of possibility and novelty
— sensing the possible
and the new.

in art history at Ohio State.
I study comics and painting
through the lens of modernism.
